3. Monday Night Football Tiebreaker

Predict total points in Monday Night Football. Closest prediction breaks ties for weekly and season standings.

4. Scoring System

Earn your confidence points for each correct pick. Wrong picks earn zero points. Highest weekly and season totals win.

8. Survival Strategy

Balance between strong picks early and saving good teams for later weeks. Last person standing wins!

9. Elimination Rules

Your chosen team must WIN outright. Ties count as losses. Eliminated players can still play confidence picks.

10. Final Survivor

If multiple players survive the full season, the winner is determined by best overall confidence pick record.
